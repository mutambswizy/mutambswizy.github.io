Our School

Direct Contact School is an english medium school that develops cultural learners meant to fit in top echelons of society. The school was established to provide elite education in a christian medium. The School offers Cambridge examinations from checkpoint 1 to checkpoint 6 as well as zimsec examinations. We provide a holistic approach of the curriculumn and pride ourselves in our effective remedial program which ensures that no child is left behind. We are registered with the Ministry of Primary and Secondary Education
